Exhibition 1: Echoes of the Digital Mind

This exhibition explores how artificial intelligence interprets human creativity. Visitors can interact with installations where AI systems generate paintings, music, and poetry in real time based on audience input. The exhibit invites guests to question where creativity begins—within the machine, the artist, or the collaboration between both.

Exhibition 2: Light, Motion, and Memory

Light, Motion, and Memory is an immersive exhibition that blends projection art, sound, and movement. As visitors walk through the gallery, sensors transform their motion into shifting patterns of light and color. The exhibition demonstrates how technology can turn human presence into living artwork, creating a constantly evolving visual environment.
